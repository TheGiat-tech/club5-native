# Privacy Policy for Club5 AI

**Effective Date:** December 11, 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 11, 2024

---

## Introduction

Welcome to **Club5 AI** ("we," "our," or "the App"). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

---

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.**

All data generated by the App, including but not limited to:
- Your name
- Wake-up time preferences
- Daily check-in records
- Streak information
- Coach selection
- Settings and preferences

...is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage (a local storage technology).

---

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage Only:** All user data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.
- **No Cloud Sync:** We do not offer cloud synchronization. If you uninstall the App or clear your device's storage, your data will be permanently deleted.

---

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share your data with anyone.**

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal information, there is nothing to share with third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use:
- Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services

---

## Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

### Android:
- **SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM** - To schedule wake-up notifications at your chosen time
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED** - To restart notifications after device reboot
- **VIBRATE** - To vibrate on notifications
- **WAKE_LOCK** - To ensure notifications work properly
- **USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT** - To display full-screen wake-up alarms

### iOS:
- **Notifications** - To send you wake-up reminders

These permissions are used **solely** for the App's core functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, as we do not collect any personal information at all.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your device's security measures (PIN, fingerprint, Face ID, etc.) protect your data
- We recommend keeping your device secure and up-to-date
- Uninstalling the App will delete all local data permanently
