Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 11, 2024
Last Updated: December 11, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Club5 AI ("we," "our," or "the App"). Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data generated by the App, including but not limited to:

  • Your name
  • Wake-up time preferences
  • Daily check-in records
  • Streak information
  • Coach selection
  • Settings and preferences
  • Chat history with AI coach
  • Progress and achievements

...is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage (a local storage technology built into React Native).

Data Storage

Local Storage Only

All user data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

No Cloud Sync

We do not offer cloud synchronization. If you uninstall the App or clear your device's storage, your data will be permanently deleted.

Data Persistence

Your data persists on your device until you:

  • Uninstall the App
  • Clear the App's data/cache
  • Reset your device to factory settings

Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with anyone.

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal information, there is nothing to share with:

  • Third parties
  • Advertisers
  • Analytics services
  • Marketing companies
  • Other users
  • Anyone else

Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services
  • Crash reporting services (e.g., Crashlytics, Sentry)
  • Any other third-party tracking or data collection tools

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to function properly:

Android Permissions:

  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM - To schedule wake-up notifications at your chosen time
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED - To restart notifications after device reboot
  • VIBRATE - To vibrate your device on notifications
  • WAKE_LOCK - To ensure notifications work properly even when device is sleeping
  • USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT - To display full-screen wake-up alarms

iOS Permissions:

  • Notifications - To send you wake-up reminders at your chosen time

These permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality and do not involve any data collection or transmission.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, as we do not collect any personal information at all.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your device's security measures (PIN, fingerprint, Face ID, pattern lock, etc.) protect your data
  • We recommend keeping your device secure and up-to-date with the latest security patches
  • Uninstalling the App or clearing app data will delete all information permanently
  • There is no way to recover data once deleted, as we have no backups or cloud storage

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be:

  • Posted on this page
  • Announced within the App (if significant)
  • Effective immediately upon posting

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App's interface
  • Delete: Uninstalling the App or clearing app data will delete all information
  • Export: Currently not available, as data is stored locally only
  • Portability: Future versions may include data export features

International Users

Since we do not collect, transmit, or store any data on servers, data protection regulations such as GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), and others do not apply to our data handling practices. Your data never leaves your device.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union: Since we do not process any personal data on servers, GDPR processing requirements do not apply to our App's data handling.
